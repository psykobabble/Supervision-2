Critically Reflective Supervision

What's this all about?
This is a collection of documents, links, quizzes and other support materials for critically reflective supervision. You should have completed the first module in Reflective Supervision before starting on the work here.
Issues in Supervision
You will look at a case study that illustrates how supervision practices impact on service users. You and your supervisor will then critically explore your supervision identifying strengths and areas for development. Use the Completed button below to setup an email reminder for your supervisor to confirm that this topic is complete.

Simulated Supervision
We will be using some examples of simulated supervision practice to explore a variety of models of the supervision relationship. We will also address issues in supporting the performance of others, challenging ourselves, managing workloaads and being accountable and taking responsibility.

Readings and Research
This topic focusses on research and theoretical material that supports our understanding of the function and nature of supervision.

Training and Development Plan
Once you have completed the other topics you and your supervisor will to discuss your ongoing training and development needs. Use the Completed button below to setup an email reminder for your supervisor to confirm that you have agreed your training and development plan.
